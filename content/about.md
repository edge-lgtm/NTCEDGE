The **Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE)** System is the flagship digital initiative of the National Telecommunications Commission (NTC). It represents a significant milestone in our ongoing commitment to digital transformation and the Ease of Doing Business (EODB) Act.

## Our Mission
To deliver transparent, efficient, and accessible regulatory services through advanced digital platforms, ensuring that the Philippine telecommunications sector remains competitive and compliant with national standards.

## Purpose of the System
NTC EDGE was developed to modernize the end-to-end processing of regulatory applications. By leveraging digital technology, we have streamlined workflows from initial submission and technical evaluation to the final issuance of certificates and permits.

## National Telecommunications Commission
The NTC is the primary government agency responsible for the regulation and supervision of telecommunications services, broadcast operations, and radio spectrum management in the Republic of the Philippines. We are dedicated to protecting public interest while fostering a dynamic environment for telecommunications growth.

## Public Service Commitment
We are committed to providing the highest quality of service to our stakeholders. The NTC EDGE Portal ensures that regulatory processes are conducted with the utmost integrity, speed, and accountability.
