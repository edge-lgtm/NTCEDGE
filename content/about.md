## Our Mission
To provide a fast, transparent, and accessible digital platform for all telecommunications-related regulatory services in the Philippines.

## What is EDGE?
The **Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE)** System is the NTC's primary digital transformation project. It is designed to handle the end-to-end process of applications, from submission to evaluation and issuance of certificates.

## National Telecommunications Commission
The NTC is the government agency responsible for the regulation and supervision of telecommunications services, broadcast operations, and radio spectrum management in the Philippines.
