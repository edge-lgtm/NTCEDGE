---
title: "Handling of Complaints and Requests"
category: "Other Public Transactions"
icon: "complaints"
featured: false
description: "Official channel for submitting complaints, requests for information, and other public concerns regarding telecommunications and broadcast services."
eligibility: "All citizens, consumers, and entities who have concerns regarding telecommunications services, broadcast content, or radio interference."
processing_time: "3-15 Working Days"
fees: "Free of Charge"
requirements:
  - "Formal letter of complaint or request addressed to the Commission"
  - "Evidence or documentation supporting the complaint (e.g., billing statements, screenshots)"
  - "Valid Government-issued Identification of the complainant"
  - "Proof of prior attempt to resolve the issue with the concerned service provider"
steps:
  - "Submission of formal complaint or request via the NTC EDGE Portal"
  - "Initial review and categorization of the concern by the Public Assistance Desk"
  - "Referral to the concerned department or regional office for technical/legal evaluation"
  - "Coordination with the concerned telecommunications provider or entity (if applicable)"
  - "Official response or resolution issued by the NTC"
  - "Case closure and applicant notification"
---
The National Telecommunications Commission is dedicated to protecting the rights of consumers and ensuring the quality of telecommunications services in the Philippines. Through the NTC EDGE Portal, citizens can easily file complaints and submit requests, ensuring a transparent and accountable resolution process.
