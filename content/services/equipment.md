title: "Equipment Registration"
category: "Equipment / Registration Services"
icon: "equipment"
featured: true
description: "Mandatory registration and certification of customer premises equipment (CPE) and various radio communication devices."
eligibility: "Authorized importers, legitimate equipment dealers, and individual end-users of telecommunications devices."
processing_time: "3-5 Working Days"
fees: "PHP 100.00 - 500.00 per unit (Administrative Fee)"
requirements:
  - "Official Sales Invoice or Proof of Ownership"
  - "Manufacturer's Technical Data Sheet and Specifications"
  - "NTC Type Approval or Type Acceptance Certificate"
  - "Importation Clearances and Customs Documents (where applicable)"
steps:
  - "Initiation of equipment registration request"
  - "Submission of technical specifications and equipment serial numbers"
  - "Upload of ownership and compliance documentation"
  - "Verification of technical standards compliance"
  - "Settlement of registration fees"
  - "Issuance of Digital Registration Sticker or Certificate"
---
All radio communication equipment and designated customer premises equipment must be registered with the Commission to ensure adherence to national technical standards and compatibility with the Philippine telecommunications infrastructure.
