---
title: "Equipment Registration"
description: "Registration of customer premises equipment (CPE) and radio communication equipment."
eligibility: "Importers, dealers, and end-users of telecommunications equipment."
processing_time: "3-5 Working Days"
fees: "PHP 100.00 - 500.00 per unit"
requirements:
  - "Invoice or Proof of Purchase"
  - "Technical Data Sheet"
  - "NTC Type Approval/Acceptance Certificate"
  - "Customs clearance (for imported items)"
steps:
  - "Select equipment registration service"
  - "Provide technical details and serial numbers"
  - "Upload proof of ownership/importation"
  - "Pay registration fees"
  - "Download Registration Sticker/Certificate"
---
All radio communication equipment and certain customer premises equipment must be registered with the NTC to ensure compliance with national technical standards.
