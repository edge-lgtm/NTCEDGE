---
title: "Permits and Licenses"
description: "Regulatory issuance of permits and licenses for telecommunications networks, broadcast stations, and private radio communication systems."
eligibility: "Duly registered business entities, government agencies, and authorized individuals intending to operate radio communication facilities."
processing_time: "15-30 Working Days"
fees: "Variable (In accordance with NTC Schedule of Fees)"
requirements:
  - "Valid SEC Registration or DTI Business Name Certificate"
  - "Detailed Network Plan and Engineering Design signed by a licensed PECE"
  - "Proof of Financial and Technical Capability"
  - "Relevant Local Government Unit (LGU) Clearances"
  - "Current Radio Station License (for modification or renewal)"
steps:
  - "Submission of comprehensive digital license application"
  - "Technical evaluation and spectrum analysis by NTC engineering departments"
  - "Review of legal and financial documentary requirements"
  - "Official assessment and settlement of regulatory fees"
  - "Final review by the Commission"
  - "Issuance of official Permit or License"
---
The National Telecommunications Commission regulates the installation, operation, and maintenance of all telecommunications and broadcast facilities. The NTC EDGE Portal serves as the official gateway for entities to secure the necessary legal authority to operate within the national spectrum.
