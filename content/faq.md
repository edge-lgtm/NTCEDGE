### How do I apply?
Navigate to the [Apply](/apply/) page, select your desired service, and follow the multi-step instructions provided.

### How do I track my application?
You can use your reference number on the [Track Application](/track/) page to see real-time updates on your submission.

### What documents are required?
Each service has specific requirements. You can view the list of requirements by selecting a service from our [Services](/services/) page.

### Where can I find my reference number?
Your reference number is provided on the success screen after you submit your application and is also sent to your registered email address.

### What should I do if my application is incomplete?
If our evaluators find your application incomplete, you will receive a notification via email with instructions on how to resubmit the missing documents.

### How do I contact support?
For technical assistance or inquiries regarding your application, you can reach us via our official hotline at (02) 8924-4008 or email us at support@ntc.gov.ph.
