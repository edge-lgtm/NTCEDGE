---
title: "NTC Launches EDGE System to Modernize Regulatory Services"
date: 2025-05-22T09:00:00+08:00
---

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) is proud to announce the official launch of the Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE) system, a state-of-the-art digital platform designed to modernize regulatory processes and improve public service delivery. As a key component of the Commission’s digital transformation and e-governance initiatives, NTC EDGE serves as the official online information system for the streamlined issuance of certificates, permits, and licenses, as well as the efficient handling of public complaints and requests. This transition to a robust digital architecture significantly reduces processing times and increases operational efficiency, ensuring better accessibility for citizens, professionals, and business stakeholders across the country. By enabling online submission and real-time application tracking, the EDGE system simplifies administrative workflows and reduces the necessity for physical visits to NTC offices, providing a more convenient experience for all users. Built with a focus on transparency, security, and reliability, the platform guarantees the highest level of integrity in every government transaction. We encourage the public and all telecommunications stakeholders to utilize this modern digital gateway to experience a more responsive and transparent government service. Bringing NTC services closer to you.
