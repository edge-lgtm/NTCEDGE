---
title: "Quarterly Renewal Deadline Reminder"
date: 2025-03-15T09:00:00+08:00
description: "Important reminder for the upcoming deadline of quarterly license and permit renewals."
---

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) wishes to remind all license and permit holders that the deadline for the **First Quarter Renewal of 2025** is approaching. All regulated entities and individual certificate holders are encouraged to submit their renewal applications through the **NTC EDGE Portal** on or before **March 31, 2025**.

To ensure a smooth renewal process and avoid late filing surcharges, we recommend using the portal's digital renewal feature. Please ensure that all documentary requirements are prepared and uploaded in the correct format.

Timely renewal of licenses and permits is essential for the continuous and legal operation of radio communication services and telecommunications facilities across the country. The NTC EDGE system is designed to facilitate these transactions efficiently, reducing the need for physical visits to our regional offices.
