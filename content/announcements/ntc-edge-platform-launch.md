---
title: "Official Launch of NTC EDGE: Advancing Digital Governance for the Filipino People"
date: 2026-05-20T10:00:00+08:00
---

The National Telecommunications Commission is pleased to announce the official launch of the NTC EDGE (Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation) platform, a comprehensive digital information system designed to modernize regulatory processes and enhance public service delivery across the nation. This milestone initiative represents a significant leap in our digital transformation journey, aligning with the national agenda for e-governance to provide a more efficient, transparent, and accessible government experience for all. By streamlining workflows and reducing manual transactions, NTC EDGE significantly decreases processing times and improves operational efficiency for citizens, businesses, and telecommunications professionals alike. The platform serves as a centralized portal for essential regulatory services, including the issuance of Certificates, Permits, and Licenses, as well as the efficient handling of Complaints and Requests. Through secure online submissions and real-time application tracking, NTC EDGE simplifies complex procedures and empowers users to manage their regulatory requirements without the need for frequent physical visits to NTC offices. We remain committed to maintaining a secure, reliable, and transparent system that fosters trust and promotes a more digitally-inclusive Philippines. We encourage all stakeholders to explore the new platform and experience the convenience of digitized regulatory services today. Bringing NTC services closer to you.
