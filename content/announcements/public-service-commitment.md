---
title: "NTC EDGE: A Commitment to Transparent and Accountable Governance"
date: 2025-04-10T09:00:00+08:00
description: "Public notice on the NTC's commitment to transparency and accountability through the EDGE platform."
---

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) reaffirms its commitment to the principles of transparency and accountability in government service. With the full implementation of the **NTC EDGE Portal**, we are providing our stakeholders with a more open and verifiable regulatory environment.

By digitizing our workflows, every step of the application process is now logged and traceable, ensuring that all transactions are handled with the highest level of integrity. This initiative is in line with the **Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018 (R.A. 11032)**.

We remain dedicated to continuous improvement and welcome public feedback as we strive to deliver world-class regulatory services to the Filipino people.
