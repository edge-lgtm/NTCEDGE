---
title: "Official Launch of NTC EDGE: Advancing Digital Governance for the Filipino People"
date: 2026-04-18T10:00:00+08:00
---

The National Telecommunications Commission is proud to announce the official launch of the NTC EDGE (Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation) system, a major leap forward in our commitment to digital transformation and e-governance. As the Commission's official online information system, NTC EDGE serves as a secure and reliable platform designed to modernize regulatory processes and improve public service delivery for all Filipinos. This new system offers significant improvements in efficiency by reducing processing times and providing better accessibility for citizens, professionals, and businesses nationwide. Key services such as the issuance of Certificates, Permits, and Licenses, as well as the processing of public Complaints and Requests, are now fully integrated into this streamlined digital portal. By enabling online submission and real-time status tracking, NTC EDGE simplifies transactions and eliminates the need for physical visits, ensuring a more convenient and transparent experience. We remain dedicated to providing a secure and trustworthy environment for all telecommunications-related transactions as we move towards a more digitally-empowered government. We encourage all stakeholders to visit the NTC EDGE portal today to experience a faster and more accessible way of doing business with the Commission. Bringing NTC services closer to you.
