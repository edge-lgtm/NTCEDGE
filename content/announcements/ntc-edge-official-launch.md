---
title: "Official Launch of NTC EDGE: Modernizing Regulatory Services for a Digital Philippines"
date: 2026-05-12T16:00:00+08:00
---

The National Telecommunications Commission is proud to announce the official launch of the NTC EDGE (Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation) system, a state-of-the-art digital platform that marks a significant milestone in our commitment to digital transformation and e-governance. As the Commission's official online information system, NTC EDGE is designed to modernize regulatory processes, reduce manual transactions, and significantly improve public service delivery for all citizens and businesses. The system brings forth a more efficient regulatory environment with reduced processing times and enhanced accessibility, allowing users to interact with the Commission from the comfort of their homes or offices. Key services integrated into the platform include the issuance of Certificates, Permits, and Licenses, as well as a dedicated mechanism for handling Complaints and Requests. By enabling secure online submissions and real-time application tracking, NTC EDGE simplifies complex regulatory workflows and eliminates the necessity for most physical office visits. Built with a focus on transparency, security, and reliability, the platform ensures that all telecommunications-related transactions are handled with the highest level of integrity and trust. We invite the general public, telecommunications professionals, and business stakeholders to experience this new standard of public service by accessing the portal today. Bringing NTC services closer to you.
