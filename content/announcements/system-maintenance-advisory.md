---
title: "System Maintenance Advisory: March 2025"
date: 2025-03-01T00:00:00+08:00
description: "Scheduled system maintenance for the NTC EDGE Portal to improve performance and security."
---

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) informs the public that the **NTC EDGE Portal** will undergo scheduled system maintenance on **Sunday, March 16, 2025, from 12:00 AM to 4:00 AM**.

During this period, the portal may be intermittently unavailable. This maintenance is part of our commitment to provide a more secure and efficient digital service experience for all stakeholders. We encourage all users to plan their applications and transactions accordingly to avoid any inconvenience.

We thank you for your continued support and understanding as we work towards the continuous improvement of our digital governance systems. For urgent concerns during the maintenance window, you may contact our technical support team via the official channels provided in our contact page.
