---
title: "Official Launch of NTC EDGE: Revolutionizing Regulatory Services Through Digital Innovation"
date: 2026-05-15T09:00:00+08:00
---

The National Telecommunications Commission is proud to announce the official launch of the Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE) system, a pioneering digital platform designed to modernize the Commission's regulatory functions and enhance the delivery of public services. As the official online information system of the NTC, EDGE represents a significant milestone in our digital transformation journey and our commitment to effective e-governance. By transitioning from manual to digital processes, the system offers substantial improvements in operational efficiency, including significantly reduced processing times and enhanced accessibility for all citizens, professionals, and business entities. The NTC EDGE portal facilitates the streamlined issuance of Certificates, Permits, and Licenses, and provides a dedicated mechanism for the efficient handling of Complaints and Requests. This innovative system simplifies the entire application process through secure online submissions and real-time tracking capabilities, effectively minimizing the need for physical office visits. Grounded in the principles of transparency, security, and reliability, NTC EDGE ensures a trustworthy environment for all telecommunications-related transactions. We encourage the public and all stakeholders to utilize this official platform for a more responsive and seamless government service experience. Bringing NTC services closer to you.
